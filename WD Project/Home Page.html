Welcome to Sweden


Discover a country where nature is always present. In our cities, in our innovations and in our minds. Our nature has affected what we eat, where we live, and how we live. Welcome to a destination of a different nature. Welcome to Sweden!

Welcome to your nature home away from home

Sweden is a land of natural beauty and harmony, where vibrant cities blend seamlessly with serene forests, lakes, and coastal landscapes. Each season brings its charm—from summer’s endless daylight to winter’s snowy beauty and Northern Lights. Rooted in sustainability, Sweden invites you to experience the tranquility and warmth of nature at every turn. Welcome home to Sweden.

Sweden's capital, Stockholm, is a vibrant city known for its beautiful archipelago, historic architecture, and cultural landmarks like the Vasa Museum and Gamla Stan (Old Town). It blends modern innovation with a rich historical backdrop, making it a dynamic urban center.

Located on the west coast, Gothenburg is renowned for its charming canals, lively music scene, and maritime heritage. The city hosts popular events like the Gothenburg Film Festival and has a laid-back, welcoming vibe, offering a blend of modern culture and old-world charm.

Malmö, in southern Sweden, is a multicultural hub connected to Copenhagen by the Öresund Bridge. Known for its sustainable architecture, like the Turning Torso, and diverse food scene, Malmö is a compact, vibrant city that reflects Sweden’s modern, eco-friendly spirit.
